Frequently Asked Questions

How are letters and words separated in Morse output?

Letters use a single space, and words use a slash / separator.

What characters does this converter support?

The tool supports A-Z, 0-9, and common punctuation: . , ? ! / - @ : ; = + ( ) ' " & _.

How do I write SOS in Morse code?

SOS is ... --- ....

Is this tool case-sensitive?

No. Text input is normalized to uppercase before conversion.

Why does decoding skip some symbols?

Unsupported characters and invalid Morse chunks are skipped by design, and the skipped count is shown below the converter.

Can I use slash and line breaks together?

Yes. New lines are treated as word boundaries, same as slash separators.
